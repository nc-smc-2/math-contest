NC(SMC)2

North Carolina School of Science and Mathematics

Math Club Competition

The NCSSM Math Club Competition, or NC(SMC)2, is an annual math competition that brings together middle schoolers across North Carolina. The competition will be held virtually this year.

More Information

What?

Fun math problems to solve individually and in teams - see schedule and rules for more!

When?

Date: 5/13/2023

Time: The competition will be from 9:30am to 3:30pm. See Schedule for more details!

Who?

Eligibility: NC(SMC)2 is open to students in 8th grade and below.

This year, students must have access to a device with internet access.

Why?

Solving cool math problems!

After the contest: Results will be shown during a YouTube livestream set for the evening of the competition.
